# Zac's Whimsy Pack
A small mod that aims to add (mostly) balanced jokers and items!

## Content

### Whimsical Cards
Whimsical Cards are enhanced cards gained through using the new Lollipop tarot card, which have a chance to evolve into a random edition whenever they're played.

### Whimsical Jokers
Whimsical Jokers are jokers that only appear when you have Whimsical Cards, and often have mechanics linked to them such as scaling off them or adding additional mechanics when Whimsical Cards score.

### Evil Jokers
Evil Jokers are jokers that only appear when you _don't_ have Whimsical Cards in your deck, and can sometimes have some sort of drawback to them.
